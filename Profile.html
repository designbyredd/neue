Profile

designby.redd is a full-service boutique creative agency that takes on branding and marketing challenges. Specialising in branding, web design and development and print design— we help companies and organisations develop and maintain their brand by communicating a consistent message to their clients through progressive design service solutions.

Areas of expertise

Branding. Logo design. Brand identity design. Brand/style guide development. Corporate stationery. Newsletters. Annual reports. Tender design. Brochure design. Catalogue design. Signage design. Digital Design. Website design and development. Responsive websites. UI Design. UX Design Content management systems. eCommerce websites.

who are we

We are all about brands. We help our clients grow and develop by harnessing good design.

We are passionate about creating brands and identities for the long term. Brands and identities that are simple and effortless yet still deliver our clients commercial objectives and ambitions.

Committed to building lasting relationships. We listen to what our clients tell us, and work in partnership with them to understand their needs. We want to be there for the long-haul, contributing in a meaningful way to their business. Real success for us is becoming a trusted partner to our clients,working with them on multiple projects and consistently delivering value.

We are based in Lichfield, Staffordshire and have been serving clients for over fifteen years.

what we do

We help startups, medium-sized businesses and corporations, achieve their commercial objectives and ambitions by creating visual expressions of who they are, what they want to say and where they are heading.

We bring brands to life through:
- Designing identities and visual language systems
- Developing brand guidelines
- Building websites
- Creating and producing print and digital campaigns

our process

Research and Strategy
We have experience in our field but not yours, your collaboration is fundamental, we must meet your world, the one you know well. We analyse it and define what you need to get to your goal.


Design
We believe that simplicity is the key to everything. We do not like redundant information and we reduce visual noise to minimum. We believe that white space between the elements creates the design as the pauses between the notes create a melody.


Develop
This is not the right place to be too technical, you just need to know that we develop with the same care and attention with which we design. Everything looks beautiful and works great, and that’s what matters.
