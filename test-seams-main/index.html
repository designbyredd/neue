Our mission is to ‘Maximise potential and enhance performance’ of all players and coaches. By helping them fulfil their potential in the sport, whether it be to play for the local club or to play at the highest level.

We support clubs throughout Cheshire, Lancashire, Derbyshire, and the High Peak. Our aim is to provide clubs with the tools to become more self-sufficient so success, both on and off the pitch, is more sustainable.

Programme History

We are currently supporting several clubs throughout the North West and North Derbyshire each of which have their own particular needs. First to sign up with us in 2017 was Buxton CC. Since then we have proudly added Marple, New Mills, Tintwistle, Hayfield, Toft, Brooklands and Macclesfield CC to our growing CV.

2021 sees Glossop Juniors and Seniors join our list of clubs and we are excited to be able to spread our support across the area meaning more and more players, coaches and volunteers have access to our unique player centred coaching methods.

WOMEN AND GIRLS

Women and Girls cricket is the fastest growing sport in the country. Being invited to support local women’s teams has been fantastic and to enhance the growth of women and girls cricket we hosted a Women’s Batting Masterclass where Derbyshire’s T20 opening batter, Natasha Allen, delivered a batting session showcasing the skills needed to perform at the top level. 

Head Coach, AJ Harris, set up SEAMS with the aim to strengthen the quality of cricket coaching delivery, from clubs to districts and county. We aim to provide a wide range of cricket coaching programmes and Specialist Clinics that not only benefit individuals, groups, teams and clubs, but also parents, volunteers and coaches.
All programmes are open to girls, boys and adults, keen to discover their true potential.

SPECIALIST CLINICS

We have also delivered ad hoc specialist clinics to several clubs including Hayfield, Birch Vale and Rainhill.

BESPOKE PROGRAMMES

As we grow and our support for the local cricket scene grows, we are conscious that we need to provide a more bespoke programme and by creating a catalogue of programmes for clubs to use across their age groups means that players have a more supportive club environment that has longevity and sustainability.
